What is a black hole?

A black hole is a region of spacetime where gravity is so strong that not even light can escape. When two black holes orbit each other they emit gravitational waves — ripples in spacetime that carry energy away. You can simulate inspiral, merger and ringdown here with simplified physics for outreach and exploration.
