About Multimessenger Visualizer
This demo uses leading-order PN-inspired formulas to produce physically-informed visual chirps and inspiral motion. The neutrino coupling terms (α, β) are phenomenological — use them to explore possible multimessenger signatures. For research-grade waveforms, load NR or GWOSC data.
